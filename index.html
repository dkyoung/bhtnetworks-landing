FAQ

Quick answers to the questions people ask first.

Is this hard to host?

No. If your internet and placement are solid, hosting is straightforward. We aim for minimal disruption to your operations.

Do you need access to my network?

Usually we only need standard connectivity for the equipment. Specific requirements depend on the deployment type and will be discussed during the survey.

Who can sign the hosting agreement?

An authorized officer/owner (or someone with documented permission from them). This is to protect you and us.

What if I want to stop hosting later?

Hosting terms include an exit process. If hosting ends, we coordinate equipment removal and documentation.

Safety & Installation Standards

Our installations are designed to operate safely within standard commercial building environments and to minimize operational impact for host businesses.

  • Standard low-voltage installation practices
  • Low-power wireless equipment designed for commercial environments
  • Industry-standard mounting hardware and Ethernet connectivity
  • Regulated Power-over-Ethernet (PoE) systems
  • Surge protectors and qualified power strips to protect against electrical fluctuations
  • Grounding practices followed where applicable
  • Careful placement to avoid interference with normal business operations
  • Compliance with basic electrical and safety guidelines for low-voltage network devices

FAQ

Answers for commercial hosts and underwriting review.

What types of businesses are a good fit?

Commercial locations with high foot traffic, frequent visitor activity, and longer customer dwell times are generally the best fit.

Is installation disruptive?

Installations are scheduled to reduce disruption and are designed to be unobtrusive within normal business operations.

What do you need from the host?

We need a suitable installation location, electrical power, internet connectivity, and occasional access if maintenance is required.

Do you provide internet service?

No. BHT Networks does not operate as an ISP and does not sell internet service plans.

Who can authorize installation?

An authorized business owner, officer, or designated signer with documented authority.

Is maintenance required?

We provide ongoing monitoring and maintenance support. Hosts only need to provide occasional site access when scheduled service is necessary.
